Because not everyone can afford a full SOC for their home lab.

This Link is a free way to play with Sentinel Logs from Azure. https://portal.azure.com/#blade/Microsoft_Azure_Monitoring_Logs/DemoLogsBlade

KQL Query Reference

First Search

search * | summarize count() by $table | sort by count_ desc

This will give you a list of Logs (tables) that you can search for to determine what is going on in your network. If you don't see the logs you are looking for then you might not have that logging turned on.
